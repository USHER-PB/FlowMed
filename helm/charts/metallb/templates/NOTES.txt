Usage:

  2. MetalLB will assign an IP from the configured pool
  3. The IP will be announced via L2 (ARP) or BGP

Example:
  kubectl get svc -A | grep LoadBalancer

Verify MetalLB is working:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ include "metallb.namespace" . }}
